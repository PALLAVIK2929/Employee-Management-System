# Employee Handbook

## Working Hours
Our standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. We offer flexible start times between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM.

## Vacation Policy
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid vacation per year. Vacation requests must be submitted at least two weeks in advance.

## Sick Leave Policy
Full-time employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. For absences exceeding three consecutive days, a doctor's note is required. Please notify your manager by 9:30 AM on the day of absence.

## Remote Work
We support a hybrid work model. Employees can work from home up to two days per week, subject to manager approval. The company provides a one-time $500 stipend for home office equipment (desk, chair, monitor) for all permanent staff.

## Onboarding Process
New hires undergo a structured onboarding program:
- **Phase 1 (Days 1-30)**: Orientation, team introductions, and basic system access.
- **Phase 2 (Days 31-60)**: Role-specific training and initial project assignments.
- **Phase 3 (Days 61-90)**: Fully integrated project work and performance feedback.

## Health Benefits
Comprehensive health insurance is provided to all full-time employees starting from their first day. This includes dental and vision coverage.

## Dress Code
Our dress code is business casual. On Fridays, we have a casual dress day.

## Code of Conduct
We are committed to a diverse and inclusive workplace. Harassment of any kind is not tolerated.

## Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year, in June and December.

## Referral Bonus
Employees who refer a successful candidate for a full-time position receive a bonus of $1,000 after the new hire completes three months.
